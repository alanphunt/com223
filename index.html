who is Too Many Kochs?

Too Many Kochs, shorthand for too many cooks in the kitchen, is probably the most synergistic group COM223 has ever seen and will ever see. Period.

Our charisma, enthusiasm, positive outlook, and determination is unmatched in everything we do. We're here for a reason and nothing can stop us from achieving what we've set out to do for Habitat for Humanity.

why we're here

Our mission is to serve McLean County by partnering with non for profits to impact lives for the better both locally and abroad. Our goal is to assist Habitat for Humanity by volunteering our time and energy in whatever volunuteer positions the organization sees a need for us to fill.

The goal is to raise $700 in the form of money or volunteer hours and the proceeds will go to building a house for a family in the spring of 2020.

what we've done

It's been an incredibly productive semester and we're grateful to have such an awesome group of people to work with that we can now call friends. We've played triva at the Firehouse, celebrated 21st birthdays, and we even won the competition to raise the most money during Habitat for Humanity's trick or treat for change on Halloween night. It was snowing and windy, but it was worth it to say we raise over $450 in less than two hours.
